How to use these sheets

Read this once. Then never think about it again.
```
🎧 MORNING — on your commute
Open Speechify (or your audio reader) and listen to the file listed while you travel. Or open the Psychomentor folder and watch/listen to the relevant video. Just listen. This counts as studying.
❓ LUNCHTIME — 25 minutes maximum
Open the exact file listed. Do the questions listed. Set a phone timer. When it goes off, stop. Quickly read through your wrong answers. Done.
📘 EVENING — one 25-minute Pomodoro
Set a timer for 25 minutes. Open the exact file listed. Read the section listed. When the timer goes off, stop. Do not push through. Rest is part of learning.
📅 SATURDAY — longer session or mock
Three 25-min Pomodoros early on. From Week 20, Saturday = FULL MOCK EXAM day. 150 questions. 3 hours. Fully timed. No interruptions. Go through every wrong answer after.
😴 SUNDAY — plan and rest only
Spend 10 minutes writing down Mon–Fri study slots. Then stop. No new material on Sundays. Your brain needs to consolidate what it has learned.
💡 WHEN YOU MISS A DAY — do not catch up
Do not double-up. Do not feel guilty. Pick up the next day's sheet and continue. Missing days is normal. What matters is that you keep going.
```
